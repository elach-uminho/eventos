Call for papers

Proposals are invited for individual papers (approx. 15 minutes) or panels (3 participants, 30 minutes) in Portuguese, Spanish or English.

Proposals should include title, abstract (ca. 250 words), 5 keywords, and affiliation and biographical note of the proponent(s) (ca. 150 words), and be sent to coloquio.mic@gmail.com by 30 November. Notification of acceptance will be sent by 5 December.
